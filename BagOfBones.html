If you are a fan of horror or the paranormal I highly recomment that you check this book out. I graduated in 1999 and I remember reading this my senior year. Rarely do I find a book where everything around me fades out. This is one of those books.

Spoiler Alert!

[Bag of Bones] focuses on an author who suffers severe writer's block and delusions at an isolated lake house four years after the death of his wife. The book re-uses many basic plot elements of Daphne du Maurier's Rebecca, which is directly referenced several times in the book's opening pages; however, the relation of these elements (including a wife who is dead as the book opens, her posthumous effect on future romance, a drowning, and house haunted by the memories of previous inhabitants) to the plot and characters is markedly different. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bag_of_Bones
